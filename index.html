              We love it when you visit, but not if its inconvenient for you.

              These days everything is available online and right at your fingertips including Sun Federal Credit Union! When you use our Online Banking, your account is always with you. You have 24/7/365 access at home, from your PC and while youre on the go from your smartphone or tablet.

              Our Online Banking makes checking your balance, paying bills, transferring money, depositing checks and more doable from work, home, and everywhere else.

              With Online Banking, you have transaction security and more control over your money.

              Easy, Safe Online Access:

              • FREE for ALL members
              • Access and enroll in eStatements
              • Withdrawals & deposits
              • Real-time balances
              • Account information & history
              • Scheduled & one-time fund transfers
              • Customize your theme and layout
              • FREE Bill Pay
              • 3-Tier security policy
              • Online message center
              • Budget and savings goal tools
              • Mobile banking & Apps
              • Text or email notifications

              1. Proof of Income
              2. Dealer Purchase Agreement (purchasing)
              3. 10-Day Payoff Letter (refinancing)
              4. Lease Buyout Agreement (leasing)
              5. Vehicle Title (clear title loan)
              6. Proof of Insurance
              7. Title Form
              8. GAP Contract

              Loan + Membership Application

              What youll need to get started
              1. Government issued ID
              2. Complete mailing address
              3. Social Security Number
              4. Date of birth
              5. A way to make your initial deposit (debit card, credit card or electronic transfer
              6. Qualify: View our list of eligible employers, organizations and communities
              7. Bundle and Save: qualify for ourMember Value Programwhen you have a savings, checking, and 1 additional product (Loan, Certificate, Money Market, or Visa Credit Card)
              What to expect when applying for membership
              1. Select accounts you want to open
              2. A savings will be automatically included, then add what you need. From Free Kasasa Checking to certificates, we have it all!
              3. Tell us about yourself
              4. Help us get to know you!
              5. Set your opening deposit
              6. All you need is a $5 deposit for your new SFCU savings account!
